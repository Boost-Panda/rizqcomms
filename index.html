What I Bring to the Table

What I Bring to the Table

Coaching

I offer 1:1 financial coaching to women looking to build a better relationship with  their finances. In debt? Not sure where all your money is going? Have savings but don’t know where to start? Click here for your solution.

Courses

I offer a number of courses covering the fundamentals of finance to investing to money in marriage - all of which are designed to help you navigate your finances. Learn more here.

Consultancy

I offer consultancy services to help you to optimise your content to entice women to learn more about your work. I’ve worked with FT, World Economic Forum and UN Women to establish their campaigns increasing financial inclusion amongst women globally. 

Corporates

I offer a plethora of workshops and masterclasses including from personal finance to pensions that are perfect for a corporate audience as part of training, employee engagement as well as broader PR and Comms strategies
